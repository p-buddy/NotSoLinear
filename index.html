NOT SO LINEAR

Collaborative Decision Making Between Doctors and AI

What, why, and how?

Hi! We are Louis and Parker, two seniors in USC's Iovine and Young Academy. we want to use interface design to remove some of the mystery around how a modern AI makes decisions. That way doctors can interact with and trust in an AI's responses and use them to better treat patients.
